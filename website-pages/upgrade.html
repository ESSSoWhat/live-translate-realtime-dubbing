Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from the app (Settings → subscription) or your account in the app. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

Subscriptions are managed through our app via Qonversion. We accept major credit cards and in-app payment methods.

Is there a free trial?

The free tier gives you 30 minutes per month to try all basic features. Upgrade anytime to unlock voice cloning and more usage.

What happens if I exceed my usage limit?

You'll receive a notification when approaching your limit. Translation will pause until your next billing cycle or you upgrade your plan.
